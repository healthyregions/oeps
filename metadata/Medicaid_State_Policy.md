CSV files are organized by **year** and **spatial scale**. For example, county-level variables from 2000 will be found in C_2000.csv.  

### Data Source(s) Description:  

Variables were obtained through the 'Status of State Action on the Medicaid Expansion Decision' indicator (https://www.kff.org/affordable-care-act/state-indicator/state-activity-around-expanding-medicaid-under-the-affordable-care-act/) from KFF, a health policy organization, through their tracking and analysis of state executive activity. The source data was updated most recently in September 2025 and accessed in November 2025.


### Description of Data Processing: 
The following variables were included from the source data:
1. Dummy variable; State has adopted state action on the Medicaid Expansion decision.
